A Tree That Can't Speak


There is such a big tree in the forest,
Like other big trees, they are ordinary,
The only difference is that he is the only one in the whole forest who can't speak.

Because he can't speak, and there are no lively animals to make his home in him,
So he seems to have been alone since childhood.
The growth of a person is also very good, he comforts himself like this.

Although he can't speak, he always writes his own poems in his heart,
Every day he lived was written in the annual rings circle by circle.
Hidden inside, there are bridges of seven colors that I saw when I was a child,
There is a white cotton jacket that he puts on every once in a while,
And there, a dandelion, the one at his feet.

The big tree can't talk, so the other big trees don't have much to talk about with him.
There was only that little dandelion, always chattering beside him,
Talk about the little butterfly she knows and the little stream she passed by.

Big tree couldn't make a sound to reply, but he always listened carefully,
At the same time also pays attention to the sun, and puts the dandelion in his shadow at any time,
Dandelion won't sweat profusely from the sun when telling a story,
That's all he can do.

However, dandelions are not the same as big trees after all.
Autumn came, and the dandelion's clothes turned lifeless yellow,
The big tree knows that when his leaves turn yellow, they will fall off and disappear into the soil.
He was worried, he didn't know if the dandelion would also wither like his leaves and disappear in the soil.

But Dandelion is not worried at all,
She is a traveller, and she flies out the seeds in summer,
Overcoming the cold winter, the next year will appear where she buried the seeds,
start the next life.

She told the big tree, I'm leaving in winter, don't miss me,
The big tree shook his head, and the dead leaves clapped like a cry.
The big tree can't bear her, although the dandelion will not disappear,
But next year she won't be here.

Where did she bury the seeds?
Will there be birds that eat randomly in the next place?
Will there be a big tree to shelter her from the wind and rain?

The big tree is very sad, but he is a tree, he can't walk, can't speak,
He can't tell Dandelion to take care of himself,
Can't ask her if she will come back in the future?
The tree could only shake its head, and his leaves fell one by one.

Dandelion is still talking enthusiastically,
She said she was going to travel around the world, the duty of the traveller,
It is to extend the footprints into the distance.

She suddenly asked the big tree, do you know where I buried the seeds?
The big tree just kept shaking his head.
Dandelion smiled and said mysteriously, "Hey, hey, it's a beautiful place.

Winter came as promised, and the dandelions disappeared.
The tree put all his fallen leaves in the place where the dandelion used to be.
She once said that she was most afraid of the cold of winter,
The big tree just thought, then I will give you all the leaves as a quilt,
Goodbye, Miss Dandelion, thank you, stay with me for a spring and autumn,
After that, I was alone again.

The following year, spring came, and the breeze brought a touch of warmth from the south.
"Hey, did you miss me?"
A voice sounded, and beside him, there was a dandelion!
Right next to the original Miss Dandelion location,
They are very similar, are they her sisters?

"Hey, look here, it's me, don't you recognize me?
Hee hee hee, I didn't expect it, I buried the seeds here.
In fact, you are my world,
I won't leave without wrapping you around. "

It turned out to be Miss Dandelion!
The big tree wrote one more stroke in the annual ring,
"The Miss Dandelion I like is back!"
